1. Inside the pod — start Jupyter
Run this inside your pod:

jupyter notebook --ip=0.0.0.0 --port=8888 --no-browser --NotebookApp.token=''

2. On your local machine — port forward
In another terminal on your local machine:

kubectl port-forward -n wenglab-vs pod/<your-pod-name> 8888:8888

3. Open in browser

http://localhost:8888
